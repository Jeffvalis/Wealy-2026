About the Asset

Vanguard S&P 500 ETF (VOO) is an exchange-traded fund that tracks the performance of the S&P 500 Index, which measures the investment return of large-capitalization stocks. VOO provides low-cost exposure to the 500 largest U.S. companies across diverse industries. It is managed by Vanguard, one of the world's largest investment management companies.
